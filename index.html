About the Event

Where Culture Comes to Life

420 Fest blends weed yoga, curated DJ-led day party energy, and brand-forward cannabis activations into one elevated experience centered around Black LA culture. This year we're projecting 100 yoga participants and 300 day party attendees — a young, multicultural, experience-driven crowd that's always sharing content. Brands are integrated into the experience, not placed as ads. Fill out the form below to apply for a vendor spot.
